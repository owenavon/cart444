Welcome!

Hello, my name is Owen Avon. I am a digital artist with a passion for web development and interaction design. I studied New Media and Publication Design at Cégep Heritage College where I was first exposed to the art of digital production. I became not only attracted to the process of design making, but also towards the reasoning behind a digital design choice. My curiosity for creative multimedia led me to the Computation Arts program at Concordia University, where I have pushed my passion for the field by creating various interdisciplinary projects. I welcome you to my portfolio website!
