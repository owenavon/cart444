SPEED

2022

The intention of this project is to emulate the notion of SPEED by the use of visual and syntactic elements. In relation to visuals, quick bursts of typography will animate on and off the screen over a given narrative. The visuals are coupled with syntactic typographic semantics in relation to the speed at which technology progresses, adapts and evolves. I chose to take this approach towards the concept of SPEED, as I want to produce a work that can be similarly interpreted by all learning types. Some people may understand I am reflecting on SPEED by the visuals, others by the language, and perhaps several may understand both.
